Most Useful Course

Among all the courses, the one that stood out to me the most was ITM 735: ICT and Diversity Management. Where most courses I am taking are very technical, this course allowed me to learn about IT from another aspect- from a human aspect. I learned all about why diversity is criucial in the IT field, as it helps informs the technologies of tomorrow.

One example that particularly stood out to me was in the development of automatic hand soap dispensers. The team that designed this was not diverse, so when the device was tested it was only tested on lighter-skinned subjects. This meant that when the machine was put into production, it only worked on those with lighter skin.

Along with many examples like the above. ITM 735 taught me that thinking about technology from a human point of view is equally if not more important as the technical components of the product. Technology must be designed for all in order to create a more sustainable and equitable industry.
